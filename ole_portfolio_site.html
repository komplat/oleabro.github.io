// 004 — About

The person
behind it.

I'm an MSc composites engineering candidate with a focus on structural design and finite element analysis. My work sits at the intersection of material science, structural mechanics, and manufacturing reality.

Three years in Formula Student taught me how to take a structure from concept sketch to carbon part under time pressure and strict regulations. Three summers at GKN Aerospace showed me what that same process looks like when lives depend on it.

I'm drawn to roles where getting the analysis right actually matters — highly loaded, weight-critical structures where you can't just add material and call it done. That's what keeps me interested in F1 and high-performance aerospace.
